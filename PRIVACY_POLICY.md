# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 2026

## Introduction

NurseSphere ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform, including our web application, mobile application, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, password (encrypted)
- **Professional Information:** Nursing license details, specialty, experience, current employer
- **Profile Data:** Bio, avatar, location, professional links
- **Migration Data:** Exam scores (NCLEX, IELTS, OET), visa status, credential evaluation progress

### Usage Data

- Pages visited, features used, time spent on the platform
- Device information (browser type, OS, device type)
- IP address and approximate location (country/region)
- Log data and analytics

### Health-Related Data

- Clinical logbook entries (case details, procedures, outcomes)
- Exam performance data
- AI interaction history

### Payment Information

- Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers.
- We store transaction records (amount, date, plan) for billing purposes.

## How We Use Your Information

- **Provide and maintain** the Service, including your dashboard, exam prep, and migration tracker
- **Personalize** your experience with AI-powered recommendations and study plans
- **Process transactions** and manage your subscription
- **Communicate** with you about updates, reminders, and support
- **Improve** the platform through analytics and usage patterns
- **Ensure security** and prevent fraud
- **Comply** with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
- Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (salt + hash)
- Sensitive data (clinical logs, exam scores) is encrypted before storage
- We use secure JWT tokens with expiration for authentication
- Database access is restricted and audited

### HIPAA Considerations

NurseSphere is not a covered entity under HIPAA. However, we apply healthcare-grade security practices to protect your data:

- End-to-end encryption for clinical data
- Audit logging for all data access
- Minimal data collection principle
- Regular security reviews

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your personal information. We may share data with:

- **Service Providers:** Third parties that help us operate the platform (hosting, payment processing, analytics) under strict data protection agreements
- **With Your Consent:** When you explicitly authorize sharing (e.g., sharing profile with an employer)
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process
- **Aggregated Data:** Anonymized, aggregated statistics may be shared for research or industry insights

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

- **Access** — Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction** — Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion** — Request deletion of your personal data
- **Portability** — Request your data in a portable format
- **Objection** — Object to processing of your personal data
- **Restriction** — Request restricted processing

### GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

### CCPA Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
- Know what personal information we collect
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell data)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

## Data Retention

- **Account data:** Retained while your account is active, deleted within 30 days of account closure
- **Clinical logs:** Retained until you delete them or close your account
- **Usage analytics:** Retained for 24 months in anonymized form
- **Payment records:** Retained for 7 years as required by law
- **Support tickets:** Retained for 2 years

## Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including Standard Contractual Clauses where required.

## Cookies and Tracking

- **Essential Cookies:** Required for the Service to function (authentication, session management)
- **Analytics Cookies:** Help us understand how users interact with the platform
- **Preference Cookies:** Remember your settings and preferences

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

## AI and Automated Decision-Making

Our AI features (clinical assistant, study tutor, diagnosis support) are designed to assist, not replace, professional judgment. AI-generated content:

- Is clearly labeled as AI-generated
- Should not be used as the sole basis for clinical decisions
- Is subject to human review and can be overridden
- Does not store or use your data to train AI models without consent
